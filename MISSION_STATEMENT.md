# Rallyify Mission Statement

Scheduling a group event shouldn't feel like herding cats. Rallyify makes multiparty scheduling fast, low-friction, and even a little fun — so the worst part of organizing anything becomes the easiest.

A host proposes times, sends a link, and gets back a clear picture of who can make it and when. No accounts to create, no apps to install, no reply-all threads. Just a decision, made quickly, with everyone's input.

The north star: every interaction should reduce back-and-forth, not add to it.
